A Tid Bit About Us

We love coffee. We also love giving back. Our community and our world is important to us here at Caring Coffee, that's why we dedicate a portion of our profits from every cup of coffee sold to both local and international charities. Our mission began in 2010 but we've been expanding ever since. We have 3 new locations in the greater Houston area as well as new stores planned to open in the Austin area in late 2020. Remember, when you buy a cup of java at Caring Coffee, you're giving back to the world.
