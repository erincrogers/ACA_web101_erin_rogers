Doing Coffee Differently

We set out in 2010 to make a coffee shop unlike any other. Instead of asking for tips to pay our baristas, we wanted to accept donations to help out charities that we were passionate about. Instead of gouging customers just to pad our pockets, we wanted to charge a fair price and donate a portion of the sale to those same charities. We wanted people to get passionate again about coffee, get passionate about people.

"Be the change you wish to see in the world."- Mahatma Ghandi

Please Donate

Proceeds from the sale of our coffee go to charity but if you feel the need to donate as well please feel free to do so at any of our locations. Our staff are knowledgeable about several local charities and are happy to assist you in finding one that aligns with your personal values.
